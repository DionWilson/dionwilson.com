Meet Dion Wilson

I spent 15+ years as a professional dancer, performing with some of the most respected companies in the world -- Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater, Dance Theatre of Harlem, and Disney's The Lion King on the Broadway National Tour. That career taught me discipline, precision, and what it takes to push a body to its absolute limit.

Today I channel all of that into three things. As the Founder of Bitcoin For The Arts, Inc, I'm bridging the gap between the creative community and the Bitcoin economy. Through DBody, I deliver elite strength and conditioning programs for professional and pre-professional dancers. And as the host of Bitcoin w/Coffee, NYC organizer for BitcoinWalk.org, and a manager of the Harlem Bitcoin Community, I'm building the local infrastructure of a Bitcoin future -- one meetup, one walk, one conversation at a time.

From the stage to the gym to the Bitcoin meetup, everything I do is about discipline, sovereignty, and building for the long term.

The Foundation

15+ Years on the World's Biggest Stages

Before entrepreneurship and Bitcoin, I built my career on some of the most prestigious stages in the performing arts. That experience shaped everything I am today.

Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater

One of the most renowned and celebrated modern dance companies in the world. Performing with Ailey defined what it means to move with power, artistry, and intention.

Dance Theatre of Harlem

A groundbreaking institution in American dance. Dancing with DTH deepened my commitment to excellence and my roots in the Harlem community I still serve today.

Disney's The Lion King

Broadway National Tour. Night after night, city after city -- performing in one of the longest-running shows in Broadway history taught me endurance, consistency, and showmanship.

"Everything I learned on stage -- the discipline, the precision, the relentless pursuit of perfection -- I bring to every client, every meetup, and every venture I build."

The Mission

Bitcoin For The Arts, Inc

A mission-driven organization connecting artists, performers, and creative professionals to the Bitcoin ecosystem. We educate, onboard, and empower the arts community to embrace sound money and decentralized technology.

₿

Artist Education

Workshops, seminars, and one-on-one guidance to help artists understand Bitcoin, self-custody, and the path to financial sovereignty.

⚡

Onboarding Creatives

Hands-on support to get artists set up with wallets, Lightning payments, and the tools to accept Bitcoin for their work.

🎨

Art Meets Sound Money

Building a community at the intersection of creativity and Bitcoin. Events, collaborations, and partnerships that prove art and sound money belong together.

🌐

Financial Sovereignty

Advocating for a future where artists own their work, their wealth, and their time. No middlemen. No permission needed.

"Artists deserve to be paid in the hardest money ever created. Bitcoin gives creators the power to own their work and their wealth."
