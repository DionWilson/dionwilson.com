Meet Dion Wilson

Dion Wilson is a certified personal trainer, performance coach, and former professional dancer. Through DBody, he delivers functional training that improves movement quality, strength, and confidence for clients of all fitness levels.

From stage to studio, Dion brings the discipline of elite dance training to every session. Clients train in-person, online, or in custom performance programs with full support and accountability.
