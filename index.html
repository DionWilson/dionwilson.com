Meet Dion Wilson

I'm a New York City-based entrepreneur, certified personal trainer, performance coach, and dedicated Bitcoin advocate. My work spans three worlds that share a common thread: empowering people to become the strongest, most sovereign version of themselves.

As the Founder of Bitcoin For The Arts, Inc, I'm on a mission to bridge the gap between the creative community and the Bitcoin economy. Through DBody, I deliver elite strength and conditioning programs for professional and pre-professional dancers. And as a Bitcoin meetup host, I build community around the principles of sound money and financial freedom.

From the stage to the gym to the Bitcoin meetup, everything I do is about discipline, sovereignty, and building for the long term.

The Mission

Bitcoin For The Arts, Inc

A mission-driven organization connecting artists, performers, and creative professionals to the Bitcoin ecosystem. We educate, onboard, and empower the arts community to embrace sound money and decentralized technology.

₿

Artist Education

Workshops, seminars, and one-on-one guidance to help artists understand Bitcoin, self-custody, and the path to financial sovereignty.

⚡

Onboarding Creatives

Hands-on support to get artists set up with wallets, Lightning payments, and the tools to accept Bitcoin for their work.

🎨

Art Meets Sound Money

Building a community at the intersection of creativity and Bitcoin. Events, collaborations, and partnerships that prove art and sound money belong together.

🌐

Financial Sovereignty

Advocating for a future where artists own their work, their wealth, and their time. No middlemen. No permission needed.
